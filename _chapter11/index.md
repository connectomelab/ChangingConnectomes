There are several studies on brain connectivity at different ages. The projects mentioned in the book can be found here:

### Preterm, young, and adolescent subjects
[Twin study of the Human Connectome Project, ages 22-35 years](https://www.humanconnectome.org/) 

[Developing Connectome Project, pre-term and early years](http://www.developingconnectome.org)

[Philadelphia Neurodevelopmental Cohort, ages 8-21 years](https://www.med.upenn.edu/bbl/philadelphianeurodevelopmentalcohort.html)
 
[Child Mind Institute Healthy Brain Network, ages 5-21 years](https://healthybrainnetwork.org/)
 
[Longitudinal IMAGEN Europe study, ages 14-22 years](https://imagen-europe.com/)

 
### Life-span data
[NKI/Rockland study, ages 6-85 years](http://fcon_1000.projects.nitrc.org/indi/enhanced/). 

 
### Old age 
[UK Biobank Imaging project](https://www.ukbiobank.ac.uk/)
